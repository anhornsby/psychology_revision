About

My name is Adam H. I’m a psychology student at University College London.


I made these mindmaps whilst revising for my first year exams in 2011. I thought they might be useful as guides for psychology revision (particularly for students at UCL).


The content and the titles of these mindmaps are taken from slides provided by lecturers at UCL and from sections in various textbooks. Evaluations and opinions are my own.


Many, many thanks to all of the UCL psychology lecturers.
